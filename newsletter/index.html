Big update: Karma, Badges, Levels, and more!

Dear community, in the past two months, we've been in our developers' kitchen and prepared the biggest Guzz update so far. It brings a lot of gaming elements to Guzz, which we would like to introduce today:

Karma and Levels

From now on, you can earn Karma points with all your activities on Guzz. Karma points are experience points that show others how active you are. If you earn enough Karma, you'll level up. Conducting sessions will give you the most Karma, of course.

Badges

For special achievements, you will get badges. To keep the suspense, we won't tell you what activities it takes to achieve certain badges. While you can see other users' badges, the triggering events are not disclosed. There's a lot to discover... üî≠

Guzz Gems

By conducting sessions as a Guru, you'll earn Guzz Gems. The longer a session was, the more Gems you get. For the time being, Gems are of sole idealistic value, but this will change. More on that in a future update. üôÉ

Starting Level

Since we're introducing these game elements a couple of months after the intial launch, we had to make a decision: Should we implement a "magical mechanism" that rewards all your past activities, or shall all users start on level one?

We weighed pros and cons of both approaches and eventually decided to take the latter route. Under the hood, we're collecting as little data as possible, so wouldn't have been able to retrace how long your past sessions actually took wihout taking crude assumptions. Plus, we would have also taken away the fun of up-leveling quickly during the first levels.

So we awarded only those points and badges you would otherwise not be able to achieve anymore, since you already performed the required actions, like uploading a profile picture for the first time. As an early adopter user, you'll also receive a an exclusive golden "Pioneer Badge" that won't be available to future users of Guzz. That's our way of showing how special you are to us! üòç

We'd love to hear your feedback on these new features on Facebook, Twitter or Guzz!

Happy guzzing,

Kiara and Tom
